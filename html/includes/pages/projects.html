Projects

IOS Press

IOS press work was a minimum viable project done in an extremely short time, due to pressing needs for the client. In spite of the short timeframe, we were able to create a stable, moderately resposive (since 90% of the users were desktop users, this had a lower priority), accessible website, with GA tracking. I was one of the two frontend developers on this project.

Royal Marsden Manual

I worked for Royal Marsden Manual website, for Semantico. This is a responsive, accessible website, with support for IE7 due to client base requirements. The team recieved positive client feedback and the project was a success. I was one of the two frontend developers on this project.
