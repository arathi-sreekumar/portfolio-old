Ness Technologies

I joined Ness on July 21st 2009 as a Graduate Trainee. I was assigned to work on Air Canada Website Project of Amadeus who was then a client of Ness, on October 1st 2009. I have since had a fast growth, to a Software Engineer by 2010. I received exceptional bonuses and high salary increments throughout my career, with extremely good appraisals.

During my tenure I gained extensive domain knowledge and UI development skills in HTML, CSS, HTML 5, Javascript and JQuery. The team worked in a agile development environment with a five week release cycle. I also worked with Google Analytics, A/B testing, Mediaplex, Custom tracking tools, revamping page flows and redesigning the website template.

I also played a part in mentoring new joinees, giving training sessions on the development process and tools used.

I left Ness on June 28th 2009 to join Amadeus as part of an internal transfer plan where the Amadeus teams where transfered from the Ness management directly to Amadeus.

Amadeus Software Labs India Pvt Ltd

I joined Amadeus on June 29th 2012 as part of a transfer in ownership of the Amadeus Labs contract team from Ness to Amadeus to be a part of Amadeus's own development center in Bangalore.

I continued in the AirCanada Website Project, with an appraised position as Senior Software Engineer, and an exceptional bonus offer and a revised pay scale.

During my tenure I worked on several critical changes such as revamping the seat selection page, changing the rebooking page flows, mediaplex tag changes, adding new baggage rules to name a few. I also helped train the new joinees and gave sessions on domain, process and tools related to the project.

I left Amadeus on 17th October 2012, when I got married and relocated to UK.

Semantico Ltd

I joined Semantico on August 5th 2013 as a frontend developer.

I worked across multiple projects for clients such as McGraw Hill Education, Society of Petroleus Engineers and Dawson Era developing responsive, accessible websites using technologies such as backbone.js, require.js and handlebar.js.

During my tenure I have been able to pick up new projects and work on new technologies with ease and deliver quality work on time. I have also shown ownership for my work and have been able to manage my time efficiently and prioritize work as needed.
